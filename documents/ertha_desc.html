Ertha is an economic and political simulation that represents Earth. Players can buy NFTs for ownership, terrain-editing, and city-building permissions of the ~260,000 sectors across the earth's surface.

During this phase of development I upgraded the assets and gameplay of terrain-editing, city-building, and in-world avatar systems. Additionally, I expanded the database API to share customized sectors of land online.
