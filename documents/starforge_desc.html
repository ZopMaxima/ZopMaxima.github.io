Based on novel ideas for the time, StarForge featured digging through precedural voxel terrain, building your own base, and gameplay from the caverns to the sky. The game is no longer supported or sold on Steam, but the communnity pages still exist for a tour.

Contributed Features:
  • User Interface
  • Consumable Items
  • Tutorial Tip System
  • Environmental Music Selector
  • Upgrading Systems for Multiplayer Support
